## 🧭 How I Work

Each case study follows the same structure:
- **Context** — what the business needed and why it mattered
- **Problem Definition** — the root cause, not the symptom
- **Approach** — what I did, what I didn't do, and why
- **Execution** — cross-functional work, tradeoffs, pivots
- **Outcome** — metrics, qualitative impact, what I'd change
